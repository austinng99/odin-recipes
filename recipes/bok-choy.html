Blanched Bok Choy

Description

It's a quick and easy one. Make this in 5 minutes or less. And it's not that many ingredients either ;)

Ingredients:

  • 2 bunch of bok choy
  • 1 tbsp salt

Directions:

  1. Bring a pot of water to boil. Once it's boiling, add salt and blanch the bok choy for 1 minute.
  2. Remove and drain. Place bok choy onto a plate.
  3. Serve.
